✨ Make It Yours

⭐
Pin Your Favorites
Pin the links you use every day so they're always at the top of the page. No more scrolling to find Bell Schedule for the 5th time today.
HOW: Click the ☆ star next to any link
🔍
Instant Search
Start typing in the search bar and results filter instantly across all categories. Find any resource in seconds.
HOW: Click the search bar or press the / key
↕️
Drag & Drop Categories
Rearrange entire category tiles to match your workflow. Put the ones you use most right where you want them.
HOW: Grab the ⠿ icon on a category header and drag
📎
Drag & Drop Links
Reorder links within a tile, or move them between categories entirely. Your layout, your rules.
HOW: Grab the ⠿ icon next to any link and drag
🔤
Text Size
Make link text smaller for more density, or larger for easier reading. Five sizes from extra small to extra large.
HOW: ⚙ Settings → Text Size → XS / S / M / L / XL
📐
Grid Density
Control how many tiles fit on screen. Compact squeezes more in, Cozy gives more breathing room.
HOW: ⚙ Settings → Grid Density
🌙
Dark Mode
Easier on the eyes during late grading sessions. Flips the entire dashboard to a dark theme.
HOW: ⚙ Settings → Dark Mode toggle
📄
Favicons & Preview Cards
Each link shows the real website favicon (Google Docs, Sheets, YouTube, etc.). Hover over any link to see a rich preview card with the resource type, category, and URL.
HOW: Hover over any link — or toggle favicons in ⚙ Settings
↩️
Reset Layout
Rearranged things and want to start fresh? One click restores the default order. Your pins and settings are kept.
HOW: Click "↺ Reset layout to default" below the search bar

All customizations are saved automatically in your browser.
Clearing your cache or switching devices may reset them.
